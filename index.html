Jeff Trent

Jeff is a technology enthusiast and lifelong learner. He loves learning new technologies and skills and is learning design, digital art, sound design, 3D modeling, and new programming languages and frameworks. With his free time he has been working on videogame design and development which he enjoys since it requires a wide range of different skills.

Jeff Trent has worked in Development and IT for almost 7 years. He has taken many roles in the field from areas in systems administration to general technical support to development.

Jeff was born in Idaho and spent most of his life in the Meridian/Boise area. Shortly after graduating high school he served a two-year mission for his church in Atlantic Canada. After he came home he met his future wife, Erinn. He soon left to study at BYU, while he studied, he worked doing IT for BYU. When his son was born he decided to move back to Idaho to be closer to family.
